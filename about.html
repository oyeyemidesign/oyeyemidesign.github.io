about

I am a Nigerian graphic designer, digital artist, user interface / user experience designer and web developer. I have freelanced since July of 2016, when I began creating cover art for artists in the music industry. Most of my works now focus on Illustration, Cover art and motion design. Notable clients includes: Dj ecool and Davido, Naira marley and Zlatan, Dremo and Mayorkun, Cute Abiola.
